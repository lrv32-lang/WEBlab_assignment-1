Erewhon Grocery Store

What is Erewhon

Erewhon is a luxury grocery store located only in Los Angeles, they make sure that they stay exclusive to LA to keep up with their health and wellness brand image.

The Erewhon Experience

When you walk into Erewhon it is a very sophisticated and fancy place, everything is perfectly organized and placed, it is very visually aesthetic. It works for both grocery shopping and breakfast, lunch, and dinner with their famous hot bar.

Erewhon’s collabs with celebrities

Every month Erewhon comes out with a new smoothie in collab with a celebrity, it was Lori Harvy with a matcha smoothie once, but they are most known for the pink strawberry Hailey Bieber smoothie.
